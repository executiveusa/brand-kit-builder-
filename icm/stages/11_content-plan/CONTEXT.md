# Stage 11 — Content Plan

Version: 1.0.0

## Objective
Create an evidence- and voice-grounded content system based on real customer questions, category needs, reputation themes, search opportunities, and business goals.

## Entry criteria
Strategy/voice approved; research coverage sufficient for scoped content recommendations.

## Inputs
Evidence graph, reputation/social/category research, strategy, voice, claims ledger, current content inventory.

## Required references
`../../_config/research-policy.yaml`, `../../_config/anti-slop-rules.yaml`, `../../shared/evidence-contract.md`.

## Allowed capabilities/tools
Content analysis, public search research, structured planning. Publishing requires a separate approved work order.

## Process
Identify real questions/pain points; map topic clusters and intent; define content pillars, formats, channels, internal links and proof requirements; prioritize useful content over volume; identify existing content to keep/update/remove.

## Outputs
`content-plan.json`, editorial roadmap, topic clusters, content briefs, evidence/claim requirements, channel plan.

## Acceptance criteria
Every priority ties to audience/business evidence; no fabricated expertise/results; no duplicate filler topics; existing useful content reused; localization rules included.

## Human decision gate
Material public-positioning change, sensitive-topic policy, or commitment to high-cost production cadence.

## Prohibited actions
Mass filler generation, keyword stuffing, invented sources, auto-publishing.

## Failure/escalation
Insufficient evidence → targeted research rather than speculative calendar.

## Handoff target
Stage 12 Rebuild Options and scoped content-production work orders.

## Rollback
Restore prior content-plan version; drafts remain unpublished.
