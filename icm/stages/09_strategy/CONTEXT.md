# Stage 09 — Strategy and Voice

Version: 1.0.0

## Objective
Create evidence-grounded brand strategy, voice, and three distinct visual territories after readiness passes.

## Entry criteria
Sources verified; readiness gate passed; material conflicts resolved or explicitly scoped.

## Inputs
Brand DNA, evidence graph, discovery answers, reputation/category findings, usability audit, claims/proof ledger.

## Required references
`../../_config/source-precedence.yaml`, `../../shared/evidence-contract.md`, governing strategy and voice prompt modules.

## Allowed capabilities/tools
Strategy and voice generation/editing through approved Brand Engine use cases.

## Process
Define audience, category, position, differentiation, promise, reasons to believe, values, message/CTA hierarchy, proof risks, voice/localization rules, and three genuinely different visual territories.

## Outputs
Strategy files, voice files, `creative-brief.html`, claims ledger, territory options, recommendation and rejected routes.

## Acceptance criteria
Claims trace to evidence; territories differ strategically; voice uses real language evidence; English and es-MX rules are explicit; no generic filler.

## Human decision gate
Direction selection when alternatives materially change brand identity.

## Prohibited actions
Fabricated proof, cosmetic territory variants, bypassing approval or rights rules.

## Failure/escalation
New evidence conflict or failed readiness routes back to targeted research/discovery.

## Handoff target
Stage 10 Design System.

## Rollback
Restore prior approved strategy/voice version while preserving decision history.
