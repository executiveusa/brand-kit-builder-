# Stage 05 — Social Presence

Version: 1.0.0

## Objective
Inventory public social channels and evaluate observable activity, consistency, positioning and content gaps.

## Entry criteria
Project/entity identity is sufficiently resolved.

## Inputs
Verified brand names, domains, known handles, public research evidence.

## Required references
`../../_config/research-policy.yaml`, `../../shared/evidence-contract.md`.

## Allowed capabilities/tools
Public search/browser/social adapters that obey platform access policies.

## Process
Discover likely official profiles; verify ownership signals; capture public profile metadata and recent observable activity within bounded scope; compare naming, bios, CTAs, visual identity, cadence and content themes; record uncertainty.

## Outputs
`social-presence.json`, account inventory, consistency findings, channel opportunities, EvidenceRecords.

## Acceptance criteria
No unverified account labeled official; activity observations dated; visual/message findings cite captures; unsupported follower/engagement claims excluded.

## Human decision gate
Ambiguous account ownership or recommendation to retire/rename a material channel.

## Prohibited actions
Private scraping, login bypass, automated posting, fabricated metrics.

## Failure/escalation
Unavailable platform → explicit coverage gap.

## Handoff target
Brand DNA, Strategy, Content Plan.

## Rollback
Remove failed-run derived findings; retain source evidence/audit events.
