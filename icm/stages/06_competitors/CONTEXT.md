# Stage 06 — Competitors and Category

Version: 1.0.0

## Objective
Identify credible competitors/category references and distinguish conventions from opportunities for differentiation.

## Entry criteria
Brand category/offer/audience is known enough to select comparators.

## Inputs
Project evidence, public research, owner-supplied competitors where present.

## Required references
`../../_config/research-policy.yaml`, `../../_config/source-precedence.yaml`, `../../shared/evidence-contract.md`.

## Allowed capabilities/tools
Public search/browser/site census on bounded comparator set.

## Process
Select comparators with reasons; inspect offer/message/navigation/visual/content/application patterns; record category conventions; identify gaps and whitespace; separate principles from protected identity.

## Outputs
`competitor-map.json`, convention matrix, differentiation opportunities, EvidenceRecords.

## Acceptance criteria
Comparator selection justified; evidence cited; no design copying; recommendations distinguish category usefulness from imitation risk.

## Human decision gate
Material repositioning that changes business category or target audience.

## Prohibited actions
Cloning competitor copy, logo, distinctive visual assets or protected identity.

## Failure/escalation
Weak evidence → mark recommendation provisional.

## Handoff target
Brand DNA and Brand Strategist.

## Rollback
Remove derived comparison output from failed run; retain evidence history.
