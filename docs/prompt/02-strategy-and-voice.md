## 8. BRAND STRATEGY OUTPUT

Before visual generation, create:

```text
brand-strategy.md
brand-strategy.json
creative-brief.html
```

Include:

- one-sentence identity;
- audience and jobs-to-be-done;
- problem and transformation;
- category and positioning;
- differentiation;
- promise;
- reasons to believe;
- purpose and values;
- brand personality and anti-personality;
- message hierarchy;
- offer and CTA hierarchy;
- proof ledger;
- cultural and language considerations;
- risks and prohibited claims;
- creative rationale;
- three strategic visual territories, each clearly different;
- recommendation with reasons;
- rejected routes with reason codes.

Do not create three cosmetic variations of the same idea.

## 9. BRAND VOICE SYSTEM

Build a complete voice file from real evidence. Specific language outranks polished language.

Required sections:

```text
WHO_WE_ARE
AUDIENCE
OFFERS_AND_CALLS_TO_ACTION
VOICE_AXES
NEVER_SAY_OR_DO
REAL_PHRASES
FOUNDER_STORIES
WINS
FAILURES_AND_LESSONS
SURPRISES
PROOF_WE_CAN_CLAIM
CONTENT_PILLARS
MESSAGE_HIERARCHY
PLATFORM_RULES
WEBSITE_UX_WRITING_RULES
EMAIL_RULES
SALES_RULES
CUSTOMER_SUPPORT_RULES
LANGUAGE_AND_LOCALIZATION_RULES
TRANSLATION_GLOSSARY
WORDS_REQUIRING_APPROVAL
CLAIMS_REQUIRING_APPROVAL
CRISIS_AND_SENSITIVE_TOPIC_RULES
BEFORE_AND_AFTER_EXAMPLES
VOICE_TESTS
```

Stop-Slop rules:

- cut filler and throat-clearing;
- use active voice;
- name the actor and specific action;
- remove vague claims, empty superlatives, jargon, canned contrasts, fake urgency, and generic startup language;
- vary sentence rhythm;
- preserve real phrases, regional language, and founder-specific cadence;
- never invent stories, proof, quotes, testimonials, credentials, numbers, partnerships, or outcomes;
- no em dash in final prose unless the confirmed brand voice explicitly requires it;
- social emojis may be used only when approved by the voice system. Emojis may never function as UI icons or design-system elements.

Score voice on:

- Directness
- Rhythm
- Trust
- Authenticity
- Density

Release floor: 42/50, with no dimension below 8/10.

Outputs:

```text
brand-voice.md
brand-voice.json
voice-examples.html
approved-phrases.json
claims-ledger.json
```
